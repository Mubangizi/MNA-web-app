About Us

MNA & CO CPA is a local Audit Firm registered as a Partnership entity in Uganda with a trading licence and the necessary registrations with Uganda Revenue Authority (URA).

Who We Are?
  • The Firm is Registered and Regulated by the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Uganda. Our major focus is on Providing high quality Audit Services at affordable or competitive fees, provide an opportunity for professional on job training and internship opportunities to less privileged especially women.
  • MNA is a Christian Audit Firm and one of the Pioneer Audit Firms to Pronounce a Christian Identity.
  • The idea behind the establishment of MNA & CO was triggered by the evident low average value value add given to Clients by most Local Audit Firms, High Fees Charged by the Internal Audit Firms and evident hardship to find a first job by majority of the graduates due to lack of experience and lack of “connection/s” in Uganda and Africa at large.
  • The firm prides it’s self with having the founders, management and staff as being Professionals.
  • Founders Members have worked in the big four Audit Firms (Ernst and Young) and in the best Local Audit Firms such as Mungereza & Kariisa, Grant Thornton Uganda and others.
  • Founder Members have exposure in Uganda, Africa, Asia and Latin America and Some parts of Europe.
  • This firm is licensed and regulated by The Institute of Certified Public Accounts of Uganda

How we work

MNA & Company is proud to be associated with professional, skilled, experienced and Motivated team of project planners, managers and research experts.

  • 01 Developing project proposals

    We develop project proposals basing on clients vision and mission statements, conduct Base line surveys/situational analysis for project areas, training needs assessments, carry out mid-term and final project evaluations, conduct program reviews and manage Projects/programs on behalf of our clients.

  • 02 Developing organizational strategic plans

    Developing organizational strategic plans, provide resource mobilization and linkage Techniques, conduct research studies and over a wide range of technical expertise in all Areas of organizational development and management.

  • 03 Designing and establishment of M&E systems

    Designing and establishment of M&E systems, project management information systems And project programming. We apply user friendly sophisticated technologies that suit our clients’ technical and financial base.
